Pet Cemeteries
in Los Angeles

Losing a pet is one of the hardest things. If you're looking for burial or cremation options in the Los Angeles area, this guide covers what's available and what to expect.

Your options in Los Angeles

Los Angeles has several established pet cemeteries and cremation services. Most offer a range of options from private burial plots to communal cremation.

Private burial

A dedicated plot in a licensed pet cemetery. A permanent, marked resting place you can visit.

Private cremation

Your pet is cremated alone and the ashes returned to you. Most families choose this for its flexibility.

Communal cremation

A lower-cost option where multiple pets are cremated together. Ashes are not returned individually.

Home burial

Permitted in some areas. Check local California regulations before proceeding.

What to look for

  • Is the facility licensed by
  • Is there a perpetual care fund to maintain the grounds long-term?
  • What are the visiting hours and accessibility like?
  • Do they offer grief support or aftercare resources?
  • What does a private cremation certificate include?
Worth knowing

In California, pet funeral and cremation facilities should be registered with the relevant regulatory authority. Always ask for a registration number before committing.

The days after
