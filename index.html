Questions

Some things worth knowing.

Is Anivo only for pets that have passed?

No. Many people use Anivo to start collecting memories while their companion is still with them. Every day is worth remembering.

Where are my memories stored?

Privately, on encrypted servers. Only you have access, and your memories are never shared, indexed, or shown to anyone else.

Can I share a memory with family?

Yes. Every memory can be exported as a beautifully designed card to share on WhatsApp, Instagram, or anywhere you'd like.

What happens if I stop paying?

Your memories stay safe. You'll keep access to the first ten, and you can upgrade again anytime to unlock the rest.
